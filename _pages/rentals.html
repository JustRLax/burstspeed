Burstspeed Drift Car Rentals

Powered By Zepka Customs

Rent a Drift Car

Rent one of our drift cars at the next Club FR event at USAIR! Whether you have never drifted before or are a highly skilled driver, we have you covered. Currently you can choose between a KA24DE 240sx Hatch or an SR20DET S14. Show up to the track and we will hand you the keys to your drift car for the day. A driving instructor will always be on site to help if needed.

If traveling from out of town, we can arrange to pick you up in the Chicago/Wisconsin area and bring you to the track. Fees may apply. (USAIR is roughly 3.5 hours from Chicago)

Rentals Include

  • Event Entry Fee
  • Transport of Vehicle to Track
  • Helmet (you can bring your own)
  • Lunch / snacks, water/drinks (let us know if you have food allergies )
  • Tires (2 brand new) for the rear. (Extra New/Used tires available for purchase)
  • Fuel
  • Maintenance if any is needed
